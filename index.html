Meet Your Doctor

Dr. Shant
Oroojian

Board-Certified Orthodontist Dr. Shant Oroojian brings a patient-first, artistry-driven philosophy to every treatment. A proud graduate of Loma Linda University, Dr. Shant is celebrated for his meticulous attention to facial harmony and natural aesthetics.

Known for minimizing extractions and preserving your natural smile structure, Dr. Shant combines advanced techniques — gentle expansion, precise tooth shaping, and meticulous alignment — to deliver results that feel effortless and look completely natural.

His calm, welcoming approach makes every visit feel like a conversation — not a procedure. Even patients with dental anxiety feel completely at ease.
