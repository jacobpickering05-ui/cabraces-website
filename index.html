Meet Your Doctor

Meet Dr. Shant
Oroojian

Board-Certified Orthodontist Dr. Shant Oroojian brings a patient-first, artistry-driven philosophy to every treatment. A proud graduate of Loma Linda University, Dr. Shant is celebrated for his meticulous attention to facial harmony, natural aesthetics, and personalized care.

Known for minimizing extractions and preserving your natural smile structure, Dr. Shant combines advanced techniques — gentle expansion, precise tooth shaping, and meticulous alignment — to deliver results that feel effortless and look completely natural.

Even if you experience dental anxiety, Dr. Shant's calm, welcoming approach makes every visit feel like a conversation — not a procedure.

Signature California Braces

Our sleek, perfectly proportioned metal brackets are a patient favorite. Choose from 20+ color combinations at every visit. Advanced low-force wires move teeth gently, efficiently, and virtually pain-free.
